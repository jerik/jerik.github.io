Arnis 12 Strike Sequences

With pushing a button, a voice will speak the numbers of strikes sequenz, described on the button. The strikes are randomly shuffled.
With the sliders you can configure the
  • REPETITION of strikes, e.g. 3 times sequenz 1-5 (if you push button 'Strikes 1-5')
  • PAUSE between the strikes, e.g. 4 seconds

Works only on supported browsers. Should be Firefox, Chrome, Edge & Safari
